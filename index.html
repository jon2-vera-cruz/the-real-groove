Musician | Band

An established entity in the music business, the band, ever fresh and evolving, continues to conquer Manila 's live entertainment scene as they groove to music of proven classics of every genre, sure to strike the chord that gives one that intoxicating dose of nostalgia and euphoria.  

Early in their career the ten piece group immediately gained solid footing in the industry, marked by the release of their first album, concerts, provincial tours, television, and radio performances, magazine covers, and sold out concerts. 

Unforgettable performances in every major stage and concert venue in the country, with their unique look and very distinct unrivaled sound, produced only by a concoction of real passion for music and true God given talent that never fails to satisfy and leave audiences clamoring for more, making them a favorite for every kind of event imaginable.  

After a long hiatus from recording, The RealGroove is currently working on their second album boasting tracks sure to surprise and delight all.
